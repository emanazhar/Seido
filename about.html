About Us



Seido Technical Consulting provides essential project management services to small to medium sized companies world-wide.

who are we?

Our story begins with a group of Engineering majors that got together to solve a looming problem in the medical device industry. The result was a state of art laser system for cutting coronary stents. We branched out to more general fields as we started to notice that we would get interest not just for our turnkey equipment, but for our experience and ability to work across disciplines. Small-to-medium size businesses often experience the same challenges as much larger companies, but lack the resources, ability and mostly focus to resolve those issues in-house. Our goal at Seido Technical Consulting is to help small to medium sized companies resolve technical issues in a cost-effective manner. We leverage our diverse backgrounds and industry knowledge to offer best suited solutions that work. We’ve got business experience from start-ups to the executives of multinationals. It’s an attitude that’s taken us into a wide range of fields, in support of customers around the world.
